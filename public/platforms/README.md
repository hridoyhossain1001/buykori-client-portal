# Platform brand assets

These marks are stored locally so the portal has no runtime dependency on a
third-party CDN.

- `meta.svg`: Meta company mark from the Meta Brand Resource Center.
- `facebook.svg`: Facebook app mark from the Meta Brand Resource Center.
- `tiktok.png`: TikTok icon from TikTok for Developers' official Logo Pack.
- `ga4.svg`: Google Analytics product mark per Google's Brand Resource Center.
- `google-ads.svg`: Google Ads product mark per Google's Brand Resource Center.
- `x.svg`: X mark from the official X Brand Toolkit.

They are used only to identify integrations. Names and marks remain trademarks
of their respective owners.
